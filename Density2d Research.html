The student population density alone does not represent the overall residential population density of the region, nor can it reflect the relationship between different types of population density. Therefore, we used the kernel density calculation method to pair the student residential population density and family household density of each neighborhood.

This series of diagrams analysis can reflect whether the neighborhood is dominated by student population or family population. The darker the color, the more concentrated the number of plots in this density interval; otherwise, the fewer plots in this density interval.
